Adjustment of Status (I-485 Draft Preparation)

An informational guide to help you gather essential details for preparing your I-485 application package. So that you don't have to pay $1000s to the lawyers!

Overview

Form I-485, Application to Register Permanent Residence or Adjust Status, is a critical step in the U.S. immigration process. Properly completing and submitting this form, along with required evidence, can be time-consuming. This website aims to simplify the preparation of certain documents—specifically, a cover letter and exhibit list—to include in your I-485 package.

By filling in the details below and clicking “Submit,” you can generate a draft letter in PDF format (hosted on Overleaf) that outlines the items you’re including in your application package. We strongly recommend cross-checking the auto-generated list and attaching additional evidence pertinent to your specific situation.

Important Disclaimer: This tool is for guidance only and does not substitute legal advice. Always verify USCIS official resources or consult an immigration attorney for personalized assistance.

How to Use This Tool

  1. Complete the form below, ensuring accuracy of all details.
  2. Upon clicking “Submit,” you will be directed to sign in to your Overleaf Account to view/edit a generated LaTeX project. This project contains:
    • A draft I-485 cover letter addressing USCIS.
    • An exhibit list that organizes common supporting evidence.
  3. Review, edit, or add any missing information. Then compile the project into a PDF.
  4. Print and place the PDF cover letter and exhibit list on top of your supporting documents in your I-485 application package.

Tip: If you encounter a popup-blocking issue, please use Google Chrome or allow popups on your current browser.
